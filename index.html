Idea, Innovation, Vision and Challenges in New Bangladesh

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh thrives on innovative solutions to drive growth and development. By embracing new ideas and technology, the country is paving the way for a better future.

Sustainability

Ensuring the long-term prosperity of our environment and resources, Bangladesh is focused on achieving economic growth without compromising the well-being of future generations.

Infrastructure

Modernizing and expanding infrastructure is at the heart of Bangladesh’s vision, creating opportunities for connectivity, trade, and development across all sectors.

Empowerment

Empowering individuals and communities is vital to building a strong, equitable nation. Bangladesh prioritizes education, entrepreneurship, and inclusivity to uplift every citizen.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Innovation drives progress, sustainability ensures a lasting impact, infrastructure forms the foundation, and empowerment unlocks potential. Together, these pillars enable a future where every individual thrives. Bangladesh 2.0 envisions a nation built on creativity, resilience, and inclusivity, paving the way for global leadership and a prosperous, equitable society for all.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's of Bangladesh
